📖 How to Play Blackjack
01
🎯 The Goal
Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 without going over ("busting"). Cards 2-10 are worth face value. Face cards (J, Q, K) are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11.

02
💰 Placing Your Bet
Before cards are dealt, click chips to place your bet. Click Deal when ready. Your minimum bet is $1 and you can bet as high as your balance allows. Use Last Bet to quickly replay your previous wager.
03
🃏 The Deal
You receive 2 cards face-up. The dealer gets 2 cards — one face-up, one face-down (the "hole card"). If your first two cards total 21, that's a Blackjack and you win 1.5x your bet!
04
🎮 Your Actions
Hit — Take another card.

Stand — Keep your current hand and end your turn.

Double Down — Double your bet and receive exactly one more card. Best used on 9, 10, or 11.

Split — If you have two cards of the same value, split them into two separate hands, each with an equal bet.
05
🤖 The Dealer's Turn
After you stand, the dealer reveals their hole card. The dealer must hit on 16 or less and stand on 17 or more. If the dealer busts, you win! If both you and the dealer have the same value, it's a Push — your bet is returned.
